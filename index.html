Random facts about animals

Chicken Facts



There are more chickens on Earth, than there are humans.

There are also more chickens than any other bird species.

As chickens grow older, they lay less eggs, but the eggs are bigger

Duck Facts



Ducks are related to swans and geese

Ducks are omnivores, they will eat just about anything

Most common duck species is the Mallard, They live up to 10 years in the wild, and 8+ years when domesticated

Snake Facts



There are over 3000 species of snakes in the world. In Australia, there are 140 species of land snake, and 32 species of sea-snakes

Snakes are not aggressive in nature, but are rather shy and secretive. They will only bite as a last resort

Snakes are solitary animals, they do not live in families or groups. Snakes will however come together during mating seasons
